Que sera, sera !

We often go about in our lives unaware of so many beautiful things happening around us. Engaged in a pathetic lifestyle and caught in a box where we keep hitting the walls, we miss the smell of the morning grass or the grandeur of the setting sun. In an era of where superficiality clouds out that which is close to the heart, think of having a friend who brings all back to you. A friend who takes you away from this ruckus and lets you enjoy those serene moments that are close to the heart. Well, HoppIn is such a friend. It's different because it is for you. It is your own. It brings to you that very serenity that you have been missing.

HoppIn ensures that you get the very best for your own self. Waking up each day, heading out to battle the world, HoppIn as a friend follows you wherever you go to stand fast with you in your struggle. While you are out there fighting, HoppIn preserves for you what matters so that you can return to experience that. A platform that reserves in advance that very piece of cultural serenity that we were talking about. So that even in the hustle and bustle of today's era, that which is substantial, may it be a concert or a small social gathering, those small things which are of utmost importance are not lost. This is "the" thing. Forget about socialising. This is cultural socialising.

So Que sera, sera. These are the words of an old Italian song which means 'whatever will be-will be'. Let's live the moment forgetting all that does not matter. That is what HoppIn intends to do for you. It's about now. Right now what joy you can receive must not be denied to you. That is the spontaneity on which HoppIn works.

"Que sera, sera. The future's not ours to see. Que sera, sera. "
